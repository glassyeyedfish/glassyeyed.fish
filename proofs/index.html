The Rules of Inference

These are a list of valid steps that can be made in a proof.

From:
  • P derived.
Obtain:
  • P derived.

From:
  • If P, then Q derived.
  • P derived.
Obtain:
  • Q derived.

Obtain:
  • P assumed.
From:
  • P assumed most recently.
  • Q derived.
Obtain:
  • If P, then Q derived.
  • P discharged.

Obtain:
  • x fixed such that C(x).

From:
  • x fixed such that C(x) most recently.
  • P(x) derived.
