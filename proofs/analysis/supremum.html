Supremum

Definition: Supremum

Given \(s \in \RR\) and \(A \subseteq \RR\):
\(s\) is the supremum of \(A\) iff \(s\) is an upper bound of \(A\) and for all \(m \in \RR\) if \(m\) an upper bound of \(A\) then \(s \leq m\).
